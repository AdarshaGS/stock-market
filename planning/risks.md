# Risks

## Legal / Compliance Risk
Risk: Users may treat insights as advice  
Mitigation: Clear disclaimers and read-only positioning

## Data Quality Risk
Risk: Incorrect or delayed market data  
Mitigation: Source validation and timestamp visibility

## Overengineering Risk
Risk: Building too much too early  
Mitigation: Phase-based scope enforcement

## User Misinterpretation Risk
Risk: Metrics misunderstood by non-expert users  
Mitigation: Plain-language explanations and examples

# Scope

## In Scope
- Read-only aggregation of portfolio data
- Risk metrics (XIRR, allocation, concentration, drawdowns)
- Sector and asset-class exposure analysis
- AI-assisted explanations of portfolio characteristics
- Downloadable reports for user reference

## Out of Scope
- Stock recommendations or buy/sell advice
- Automated rebalancing or trade execution
- Personalized financial advisory
- Tax filing or legal guidance
- Intraday or high-frequency trading tools

## Phase Boundaries
- Phase 1: Insights and analysis only
- Phase 2: Optional alerts and scenario simulations
